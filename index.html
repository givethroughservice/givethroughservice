Give Through Service

At GTS, our motto is "Give Through Service" - and we strive to live that motto every day. Whether it's by going above and beyond customers' expectations or offering hands-on help to those in need, serving in the communities we live in is a mission we take seriously.

Video Here

In January 2017, GTS founded its service committee with the goal of giving back. Each month our dedicated staff chooses one or more organizations to support with their time, funds, and hands-on efforts. To date, we’ve been honored to serve:

Ronald McDonald House Charities of Central Texas

Caritas of Austin

Central Texas SPCA

Family Eldercare

The Lady Bird Johnson Wildflower Center

Meals on Wheels Central Texas

And more!
